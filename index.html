Our Story

Bannada Daara is a home-grown business start-up initiated by Lavanya in 2020. Based in Jyothigudde, Dakshina Kannada, we specialize in hand-stitched products upcycled from textile excesses and Indian-made fabrics.

Every piece we create is unique, contributing to a more sustainable and colorful future.
