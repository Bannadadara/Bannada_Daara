The Story of Bannada Daara

Rooted in the vibrant culture of Dakshina Kannada, Karnataka, Bannada Daara (The Colored Thread) was founded to celebrate the art of hand-stitching.

We believe in "Slow Fashion." Every product is crafted with minimal waste, using high-quality fabrics that tell a story of tradition and sustainability. By shopping with us, you support local artisans and eco-friendly practices.
