Our Journey

Bannada Daara (The Colored Thread) began with a simple vision: to bridge the gap between traditional craftsmanship and modern sustainable fashion.

Rooted in the rich cultural landscape of Dakshina Kannada, Karnataka, we focus on minimal waste. Every bag, pouch, and accessory is hand-stitched by local artisans, ensuring that no two pieces are exactly alike.
